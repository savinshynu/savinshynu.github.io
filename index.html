About Me

I am a data scientist with over 7 years of experience in leveraging statistical and machine learning techniques to derive actionable insights from complex datasets. My journey in data science began with a Ph.D. in Physics and since then, I have applied my expertise to a variety of challenging projects. My work involved automating data collection, preprocessing, and developing classification tasks for anomaly detection from big data, significantly reducing manual intervention and improving accuracy.

My technical skill set includes Python, R, SQL, TensorFlow, Scikit-learn, and advanced machine and deep learning algorithms, all of which I have utilized to create robust models and insightful visualizations. I am passionate about solving complex problems and continuously seek opportunities to apply my data science expertise in innovative ways, always striving to make data-driven decisions that deliver impactful results.
